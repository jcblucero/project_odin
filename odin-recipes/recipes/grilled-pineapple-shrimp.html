Grilled Pineapple Shrimp

Description

Pineapple and shrimp skewered then cooked on the grill. Yum!

Ingredients

  • skewer
  • shrimp
  • Pineapple
  • Garlic
  • sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Skewer shrimp and pineapple
  2. Season with garlic and sesame seeds
  3. heat grill
  4. Place skewers on grill for 2 minutes
  5. Flip skewers and cook for 2 more minutes
  6. remove from grill
  7. Enjoy!
