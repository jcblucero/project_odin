Smashburgers

Description

Burgers that are smashed!

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • salt
  • pepper
  • buns
  • cheese slices
  • toppings

Directions

  1. Heat griddle
  2. roll beef into 1/4 lb balls
  3. place beef on griddle
  4. smash beef flat with smasher
  5. flip after 2 minutes
  6. cook 2 more minutes
  7. remove from grill
  8. Place on bun with yuur choice of toppins
